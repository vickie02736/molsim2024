****************************************
Short helpfile for action REWEIGHT_TEMP
****************************************

The following arguments are compulsory: 

          REWEIGHT_TEMP - reweight data from a trajectory at one temperature and 
                          output the probability distribution at a second temperature. This 
                          is not possible during postprocessing. 
                   TEMP - the system temperature. This is not required if your MD 
                          code passes this quantity to PLUMED
